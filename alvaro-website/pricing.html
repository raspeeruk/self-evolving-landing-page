Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom pricing for multiple solutions?

Yes, we offer package discounts when implementing multiple solutions together. This approach often provides the best value as the solutions can be integrated with each other for maximum benefit.

What is included in the monthly maintenance fee?

The monthly maintenance fee includes ongoing support, system monitoring, regular updates, bug fixes, and minor enhancements. It ensures your solution continues to operate reliably and remains compatible with any changes in your systems.

Can solutions be deployed on-premise for security reasons?

Absolutely. We understand the security concerns in industrial environments. All our solutions can be deployed on-premise or on private servers to meet your security requirements. There is no need for your data to leave your network.

How long does implementation typically take?

Implementation timelines vary based on the complexity of the solution and your specific requirements. Simple dashboards can be implemented in 2-4 weeks, while more complex integrations may take 2-3 months. We follow an agile approach, delivering value incrementally throughout the project.

Do you offer a trial or proof of concept?

Yes, for most solutions we can develop a limited proof of concept to demonstrate the value before full implementation. This allows you to see the potential benefits with minimal initial investment.
